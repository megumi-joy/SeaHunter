Privacy Policy for Sea Hunter

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

1. Overview

Sea Hunter (the "App") is a tactical naval combat game. This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

2. Data Collection and Use

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) directly from our users. However, the App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

3. Google Play Games Services

The App integrates with Google Play Games Services (PGS) to provide features like Leaderboards and Achievements. When you sign in to PGS:

  • Google may collect data such as your player ID, game progress, and scores.
  • Your scores submitted to Leaderboards are public and visible to other players.
  • We recommend reviewing Google's Privacy Policy for more details.

4. Offline Mode

In Guest Mode or Offline P2P mode, no data is transmitted to our servers or Google Play Games Services.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing us your personal information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through the Google Play Store developer contact info.
